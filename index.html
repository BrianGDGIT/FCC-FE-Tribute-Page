John Carmack's Life According to Wikipedia:

  • Attends University of Missouri-Kansas City
  • Drops out after two semesters to become a freelance programmer
  • Hired by Softdisk.
  • 1991 - Co-founds id Software
  • 1993 - Doom is released.
  • 1996 - Quake is released.
  • 1996 - #2 on Gamestop's most influential people in computer gaming of all time.
  • 1997 - Quake II released.
  • 1997 - #7 on Computer Gaming World's most influential people of all time list.
  • 2000 - Founds Armadillo Aerospace.
  • 2001 - Becomes the fourth person to be inducted into the Academy of Interactive Arts and Sciences' Hall of Fame.
  • 2002 - Named by MIT as one of the top 100 innovators in the world under the age of 35.
  • 2007 - Wins two Emmy Awards - As a part of id Software becomes first independent game developer to win an Emmy.
  • 2008 - Carmack's Armadillo Aerospace wins Lunar Lander Challenge X-Prize of $350,000.
  • 2016 - BAFTA Fellowship Award
